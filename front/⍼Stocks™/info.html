The information about the stocks

There are 3 main types of stocks: ↹(lr), ᛤ(sh) and ⋢(ed) stocks.

The first one is the main one, and the arrows in the name of the stock live up to the title.

The second one is the most recent one, and since most of the users joined in the old times, this
stock isn't used as much.

The third one was added with the first one in the Alpha development, and gets more attention than
the newer one.

But there's a fourth one, the ⍼(dl) stock. This one is used for exchanging between staff.
